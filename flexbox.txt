FLEXBOX

- clean way to arrange items within a container
- no floats
- responsive and mobile friendly
- positioning child elements margin 
do not collapse with margins of its contents
- order of elements can easily be changed without editing source of
HTML
- ability to alter item width & height to best fit in its containers 
availble free space
- flexbox is direction-agnostic
- built for small-scale layouts while 
- upcoming "grid" specification is for more large scale

Main flex properties
1. display: flex | inline-flex;
2. flex-direction: row | column;
3. flex-wrap: wrap | nowrap | wrapreverse
4. flex-basis: <length>
5. justify-content: center | flex-end| flex-start
6. align-items: center | flex-end| flex-start
7. align-content: center | flex-end| flex-start
8. flex-grow: <number>
9. flex-shrink: <number>
10. flex: <integer>
11. order: <integer>

Flex-direction
- vertically: column
- horizontally: row
